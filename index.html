Welcome to Blue Hen Dental!
Dr. Seyar Baqi and his friendly dental team welcome you to our dental office, located in Smyrna, Delaware. We are pleased to see patients from Smyrna and all surrounding communities.
We offer a scope of general and cosmetic dentistry with expertise in preventative dentistry, dental cleanings, restorative dental procedures, root canals, non-surgical gum infection therapy, tooth extractions, dentures, and many other dental treatments. Our goal is to make your dental visits comfortable, affordable, convenient, and informative - with a modern dental team you can trust.
