Welcome to Blue Hen Dental

Our wonderful dental team welcomes you to our modern dental practice, conveniently located in Smyrna, DE and open Six days a week. We are pleased to serve patients from Smyrna, and all surrounding communities, including Dover, Middletown, Cheswold, Newark, Townsend, and other areas of Delaware.

We are proud to offer comprehensive dentistry, which includes cosmetic dentistry, dental fillings, dental cleanings, implant placements and restorations, root canals, non-surgical gum infection therapy, extractions, full mouth rehabilitation, dentures, and many more. We are proud to utilize our ivy league education to bring your the highest quality dentistry, while keeping your visits comfortable, affordable, and convenient.
